Mayakovskaya

Moscow, Russia

A fine example of pre-World War II Stalinist Architecture and one of the most famous Metro stations in the world. It's well known for its 34 ceiling mosaics depicting "24 Hours in the Land of the Soviets". During World War II, it was used as a command post for Moscow's anti-aircraft regiment. The metro station opened on 11 September 1938.
