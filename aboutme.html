About Me



I am a recent graduate from the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, having a four year Bachelor of Science degree from the Department of Mathematics and Statistics. I majored in Mathematics and Scientific Computing. My research interests include Data Analysis, Bayesian and Numerical Computations, Stock Market Analysis, Mathematical Modelling and Differential Equations. I have research experience working in India and Russia. Currently, I am working in Tredence Inc. as a Business Analyst for the Fortune 1 Company. My other areas of interest includes History, Political Science, Geography and Microeconomics. In free time, I like to write poems, read books, sing on karaoke, or play synthesiser. Apart from these, I am also interested in photography. I was born in a small town - Daltonganj, JH.

I love travelling. The Indian states and territories I have covered till date are the capital city New Delhi, Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, West Bengal, Assam, Meghalaya, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Karnataka and Kerala. Being a research fellow at the Tomsk Polytechnic University, I also covered few parts of Russia, including Moscow, St. Petersburg and the Siberian city of Tomsk. Long jouneys excites me.
