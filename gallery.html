First departmental trip. This was one of the most exciting trips I have ever been to. We did Bungy Jumping, River Rafting, Riverside Camping, Played Pranks and new relationships nurtured. Kudos to MTH fellas!

Reflection of the Dawki

Tamabil, India - Bangladesh Border

The picture is taken from a boat on the river Dawki. Dawki is one of the cleanest river in the world. You can see the fishes and other water animals swimming inside the river. Reflection on the surface is close to null. I think it is purer than the water from Eureka-Forbes ROs :P

In the Jungles of Meghalaya

Some forest near Cherrapunji

Remember Cherrapunji in our GK books? Yes it's that one only!

It's the secondmost moist place in the world. You will feel the water on your face while roaming in these dense rainforests. It was a full family jungle safari. We explored multiple natural caves and wild fauna.

The Bramhaputra

Guwahati, Assam

I was on a family trip to Assam and Meghalaya. We took a steamer to visit the island temple in the mist of this beautiful river.

I took this picture while returning back to he shore. It was around 5 pm, just few minutes to sunset and the reflection and the wooden boat mesmerised me.
